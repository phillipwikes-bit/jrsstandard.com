Justification Review Standard
Version 1.0 | AI Documentation Risk Control
Issued as part of the JRS Documentation Control Framework. Control Owner: Documentation Governance Function.
JRS™
Scope
Applies to documentation generated by AI systems and human operators.
Executed prior to any record being finalized, stored, or relied upon.
Context
AI produces explanations.
That does not mean they hold.
Records are saved.
Later, they must be defended.
Standard Requirement
The justification must stand on its own.
It must be based on something observable.
It must be clearly explainable.
If it does not, do not finalize.
Verification Check
Does it stand on its own?
What is it based on?
Could someone else understand it without you?
Control Enforcement
Records that fail this standard must not be finalized.
Embed enforcement in pre-save validation where possible.
Audit and Evidence
Evidence of compliance must be retained.
Records without evidence may be subject to audit exception.
Failure Example
Not a strong cultural fit.
No defined criteria.
No observable basis.
This does not meet the standard.
