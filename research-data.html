Research Data Room

A consolidated, de-identified view of everything the JRS platform records: challenge responses, extended reviews, automated engine reviews, poll votes, free-text responses, observation and survey submissions, findings, and the study registry. Each dataset can be exported as CSV or JSON. Names and email addresses are never shown here: contact identifiers stay in private tables. Data is observational and from a validation-phase program; it is not validated and makes no claim of effectiveness. Where participants gave consent, consent flags are included.
