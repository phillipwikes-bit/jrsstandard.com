01 · The Opportunity

A standard for decision defensibility.

Organizations are asked to defend decisions, not documents. When a consequential decision is challenged long after it is made, the record becomes the only witness, and if the record cannot explain why the decision was made, exposure follows.

JRS identifies that gap before it becomes permanent. It is a pre-finalization review standard that evaluates whether a record can still explain the basis, reasoning, evidence, and chronology behind a consequential decision. The risk it names, Decision Reconstruction Risk, sits upstream of audits, appeals, investigations, regulatory review, and litigation, across HR, public records, compliance, internal audit, investigations, and AI governance.

02 · What JRS Is

A method, an instrument, and a brand.

JRS evaluates a record against five conditions, then resolves them into a single calibrated judgment, the Decision Defensibility Score (1 to 5). It judges whether the record can explain the decision, not whether the decision was correct or lawful. That scoping is deliberate and is what keeps the claims defensible.

The five conditions
Reconstructability, Basis Identification, Chronology, Decision-Process Traceability, Evidentiary Sufficiency.
The Score
A 1 to 5 rating of how well a record alone explains a decision. The teachable, licensable instrument.

04 · Validation Status (stated plainly)

Operational validation, in progress.

JRS is in an operational validation phase. No proven-effectiveness claim is made. The evidence is built on staged rungs, each a separate, honestly reported claim:

  • R1Reproducibility: do independent AI models from different vendors apply JRS the same way. Reported: 84% cross-vendor agreement across 15 constructed records.
  • R2Reliability: do independent human reviewers reach the same judgment, measured with chance-corrected agreement. Reported: Gwet's AC1 0.74 (experts) and 0.63 (reviewers), clearing the pre-registered floor.
  • R3Accuracy: does a read match a held-out, independently verified answer key. In progress.
  • R4Criterion validity: do JRS reads correspond to documented real-world outcomes in public cases, scored blind to the outcome. Cases accruing.
Statistics are pre-registered and gated. Reproducibility and reliability are now reported with their bounds; accuracy and criterion-validity figures remain withheld until thresholds are met, and the default is to show nothing rather than a thin number. Methodology is advised by Ubayet Hossain, FRM, a model-validation professional (KPMG). The integrity controls on the dataset (gated writes, attribution, de-identification screening) are in place so the evidence is defensible in diligence.
05 · The Reviewer Panel

An independent, international bench.

The validation program is carried by a panel of credentialed reviewers recruited across professional networks, not by the founder alone. That independent bench is one of the hardest parts of this asset to copy: it is the source of the reliability and accuracy evidence, and it travels with the sale.

Scale
26 credentialed reviewers registered across roughly a dozen countries, spanning AI governance, compliance, internal audit, investigations, and records management.
Progress
7 reviewers have completed the full 24-record blind read against a held-out answer key that is independently verified 24 of 24. Reads continue to accrue.
Acknowledgment is not endorsement. Naming a reviewer reflects participation in the validation study only. Reviewers are credited as independent reviewers, never as endorsers or validators of JRS. Attribution is opt-in, each reviewer approves the exact public wording before it appears, and may withdraw before publication.
06 · The Investigator Field Guides

The standard, in a working guide.

Three finished, domain-specific Field Guides translate the five conditions into the day-to-day language of a working investigator: Employment/EEO, Fair Housing, and International. They are a near-term, packageable product line that sits alongside the Academy, and they widen the standard's reach beyond a training seat into the investigator's desk reference.

The Field Guide line was noted favorably on the Dewey Publications podcast. That is a mention, not an endorsement, and it is represented here only as a mention.
07 · The Academy (nearest-revenue, licensable now)

Education revenue before full validation.

Professional associations do not endorse methodologies, but they routinely approve educational courses for continuing-education credit. The Academy is built for that path: domain courses (HR, public records, internal audit, compliance, AI governance, and more) that can pursue CE/CPE approval through bodies such as ARMA, SCCE, IIA, IAPP, ACFE, AIIM, and SHRM. It is a recurring-revenue layer that does not require the standard to be fully validated first.

10 · Honest Positioning

JRS is research-informed and in active validation. It evaluates whether a record can explain a decision; it does not guarantee that any decision is correct, lawful, or will survive challenge. The value today is a credible standard, a working tool, a turnkey education asset, and a disciplined evidence program, with the upside that the validation data continues to accrue. Every claim here is scoped to what the evidence supports.
