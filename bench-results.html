Computed live from the benchmark workspace. Reviewer agreement = how often independent reviewers reached the same JRS determination on a record (reliability: a measurement-quality property, not one of the three rungs). Accuracy vs answer key (rung 2) = how often the AI engine (and the reviewer majority) matched the expert consensus. Rung 3 (validation: whether a JRS verdict predicts real-world outcomes) is separate and has no data yet. All de-identified.
