# Completion thank-you templates (LinkedIn / email, send with the certificate)

Reusable so this is never lost again. Two variants: one for the main Panel
reviewers (24-record detection study), one for Arm B / Comparison recruits.
Fill the bracketed fields. No long dashes anywhere (house rule).

Rule: only send a completion thank-you after DB-verified completion (24/24).

---

## A. Panel reviewer (detection study) completion thank-you

> Hi [First name],
>
> Thank you, and congratulations on completing the review. You worked through all 24 records with real care and independent judgment, and it makes a genuine difference to this pilot.
>
> Your certificate of contribution is attached. A few things I want you to know:
>
> - You will be credited by name as a reviewer in the write-up of this study and in any publication that follows. Your [domain/perspective, e.g. operations and records-management] perspective, from [country], is part of what makes this an international panel.

>
> Thank you again for lending your time and expertise. It is a pleasure having you on this panel.
>
> Best,
> Phillip Wikes
