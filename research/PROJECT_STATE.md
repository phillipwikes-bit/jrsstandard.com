# STATE & CONTINUITY PROTOCOL: JRS / DRR

## 1. Project Overview & Identity
- **Project Focus:** AI documentation governance and decision defensibility. Building JRS and the named problem DRR into a defensible, sellable IP asset.
- **Core Framework:** Justification Review Standard (JRS), a five-condition, pre-finalization record review. Named problem: **Decision Reconstruction Risk (DRR)**: the condition in which a record cannot explain, on its own terms, why a consequential decision was made.
- **Tone/Style:** Professional, concise, direct. No boilerplate pleasantries.
