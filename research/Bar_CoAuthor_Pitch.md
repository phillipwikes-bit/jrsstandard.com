The piece is strong on the US side: discovery, the McDonnell Douglas burden-shifting analysis, ESI obligations. What it needs to be complete is a European legal voice, and you are my first choice.

Section V opens that European frame, the GDPR accountability principle and the EU AI Act's record-keeping and logging expectations for high-risk uses.
